F.A.Q.

1. What are the advantages of subscribing to your newsletter?

Register in our feedback section to join our newsletter mailing list and be instantly alerted whenever a special occurs! Members may also be given brief glances of events to come in the near future.

2. How can you provide these products at such low prices?

Haha, we can't be giving our secrets to everyone! We will say however that our prices have and continue to remain within the lower spectrum thanks to an abundance of resources that allows us to pass the savings unto the customer.

3. How frequently does your stock change?

Fairly often, keeping up with the ever-changing tastes of our customers. That is why it is vital for us to receive feedback and accurately present products that everyone can enjoy!

4. Would popular demand over a particular item warrant a change in stock?

Of course! Nearly every week a "Customer Special" is revealed, specifically chosen because of a large demand we received for it to be in special in the feedbacks we take great pride of addressing.

5. How frequently do your specials change?

Usually in a weekly basis, though there are times in which a "flash special" occurs and a selected item is 50% off for the entirety of a day. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when this happens!

6. How quickly can I expect my order to arrive?

Usually all deliveries are next day, though particularly large orders may take 2-3 days. Contact customer servive at 800-555-8584 if you have any concerns on the shipping date of your order.

7. How would I contact the customer service department?

Call them directly at 800-555-8584 for any concerns you may have such as feedback, shipping dates, and such.

8. Who is (or are) the chef(s) responsible for the dishes sold here?

We are unable to divulge such information to the public at the moment at the request of the chef(s). Nevertheless, any feedback regarding our food products would greatly be appreciated in order to assure a high quality experience for everyone.

9. Do you cater?

Yes, we do! Let us know at least a week in advance in order to prepare the food however. Also, meals previously sold may be available so be sure to ask if there was something in particular you enjoyed eating in the past!

10. Who is the character on the logo?

Meet our mascot Paper Jack, the jester dedicated in giving people the basic joys in life at paper thin prices! Accidentally his guilty pleasure is that he enjoys eating pepper jack cheese with just about anything.
